# ProductHunt Listing

## Tagline:
AI office assistant that learns your style & never touches the cloud

## Description:

🤖 **AI Office Pilot** automates your emails, files, and data entry
while keeping everything 100% local and encrypted.

**What makes it different:**

1. **Three tools in one** — Email auto-reply + File organizer + 
   Invoice data extractor. They work together as one seamless workflow.

2. **Self-learning AI** — It gets smarter every day. By month 3, 
   it knows your writing style, your file naming conventions, 
   and your daily routines.

3. **Zero cloud, zero compromise** — Runs entirely on YOUR computer.
   AES-256 encryption. GDPR/HIPAA compliant. 
   Perfect for lawyers, doctors, and accountants.

4. **Works on basic hardware** — 8GB RAM laptop is all you need.
   No GPU. No expensive subscriptions. Open-source AI models.

**Built with:** Ollama + PicoClaw + Python + SQLite
**Cost to run:** $0 after purchase

## Maker Comment:

Hey PH! 👋

I built AI Office Pilot because I was tired of two things:

1. Spending 3+ hours daily on emails, file organization, 
   and data entry
2. Every AI tool wanting to send my private data to the cloud

So I built an AI assistant that:
- Runs 100% locally (Ollama + Phi-3)
- Learns my preferences over time (5-layer learning system)
- Encrypts everything with AES-256
- Has a tamper-proof audit trail (for compliance)

The self-learning part is what I'm most proud of. 
After 2 months of use, it predicted that every Friday at 4 PM 
I organize weekly files and offered to do it automatically.

It's like having a personal assistant that gets better 
every single day — and never leaks your data.

Would love your feedback! 🙏
